About Nina

I'm a PhD student in Applied Mathematics at TU Berlin, specialising in differential equations and numerical methods. I've been tutoring students in mathematics for over 4 years — starting as a tutor in my first year and building a practice of 140+ students across 8 countries.

My approach is built around genuine understanding, not surface-level exam technique. I start every student by identifying the exact gap in their foundation, then rebuild from there. My students consistently improve by a full grade within 4–6 sessions.

" I scored top of my cohort in Real Analysis and Linear Algebra. I know exactly where students get lost — and I know how to fix it fast. "
