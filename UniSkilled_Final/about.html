What we do

Two things.
Done properly.

UniSkilled runs two products on one platform. Peer tutoring — where students teach what they know. And a freelance marketplace — where students offer their professional skills to businesses.

Every student on the platform is verified through their university email. Every business is verified through their work email. Payments go through escrow — nobody gets paid until the work is done and approved.
