Terms of Service

1. About UniSkilled

UniSkilled ("we", "us", "the platform") is an online marketplace that connects university students offering tutoring services ("Tutors") with individuals seeking tutoring ("Students"). UniSkilled acts as an intermediary platform only — we are not a party to the tutoring contract between Tutors and Students, and we do not employ Tutors.

2. Eligibility

To use UniSkilled you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age, or have verified parental/guardian consent

  • Not be barred from using our services under applicable law

3. Account Registration

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and for all activity under your account. You must notify us immediately at info@uniskilled.com if you become aware of any unauthorised use of your account.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these Terms, contain false information, or engage in harmful behaviour.

  • Tutors are responsible for their own tax obligations, including income tax and VAT where applicable
  • Tutors must conduct sessions professionally, on time, and as agreed with Students
  • Tutors must not share session recording links, personal contact details, or payment links outside the platform during the initial booking process

5. Student Responsibilities

  • Students must attend booked sessions at the agreed time or cancel with adequate notice (see Refund Policy)
  • Students must treat Tutors with respect and not engage in harassment, discrimination, or abusive communication

Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU

Refund & Cancellation Policy

EU Statutory Withdrawal Right EU consumers generally have a 14-day right to withdraw from distance contracts. However, for digital services that have already been fully performed with the consumer's prior express consent and acknowledgement of loss of withdrawal right, this right may not apply. By booking a session that has already taken place, you agree that the right of withdrawal is excluded in respect of that completed session.

Cancellation by the Student

  • More than 24 hours before the session: 100% refund.
  • 12–24 hours before the session: 50% refund. The tutor retains 50% as compensation for reserved time.
  • Less than 12 hours before the session, or no-show: No refund.
  • Student did not enjoy the lesson: No refund. See "Dissatisfaction with a Session" below.

Cancellation by the Tutor

  • Tutor never showed up: Student receives a 100% refund.
  • Tutor ended the lesson significantly early without justification: Student may receive a partial or full refund at UniSkilled's discretion following investigation.
  • Repeated last-minute cancellations or no-shows by tutors may result in account suspension.

Dispute Window & Tutor Payout Eligibility

  • Students must report any issue within 24 hours of the scheduled session time.
  • If no dispute is raised within 24 hours, the session is considered completed and the tutor becomes eligible for payout.
  • If a dispute is opened within 24 hours, the payout may be temporarily delayed while UniSkilled reviews the matter.

Refund Process

To request a refund, contact info@uniskilled.com with your booking reference. Refunds approved under this Refund Policy will be issued to the original payment method through Stripe. Depending on the payment provider and the customer's bank, refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days after being processed.

If a valid refund is approved after a tutor's payout has already been released, UniSkilled reserves the right to recover the refunded amount from the tutor's future earnings or to otherwise seek reimbursement where permitted by law.

Dissatisfaction with a Session

Tutoring outcomes are subjective and depend on many factors, including the student's preparation and participation. A refund will not be issued solely because a student was dissatisfied with the teaching style or expected a different outcome.

Refunds may only be considered where:

  • the tutor failed to attend the session;
  • the tutor ended the session significantly earlier than agreed without justification;
  • the session could not reasonably be delivered due to technical issues caused by the tutor; or
  • the session was materially different from what was advertised on the tutor's profile.

Students must report any issue within 24 hours of the scheduled session time by contacting info@uniskilled.com. UniSkilled may request supporting information before making a decision. No dispute raised after 24 hours will be considered.

Payment Processing

All payments for tutoring sessions are processed securely through Stripe. Students are charged at the time a booking is confirmed. UniSkilled securely holds the payment until the tutoring session has been completed.

Tutor earnings become available 24 hours after the scheduled session, provided no dispute has been submitted within that period. If a dispute is opened, the payout may be temporarily delayed while UniSkilled reviews the matter.

Once released, payouts are sent to the tutor's designated bank account or Stripe-supported payout method. The time required for funds to arrive depends on Stripe and the tutor's financial institution, but typically takes 3–5 business days. UniSkilled deducts any applicable platform service fees before releasing the tutor's payout. A detailed breakdown of the payment, fees, and payout amount will be available in the tutor's account.

Payment Terms

Tutor Payouts

Where UniSkilled processes payments, funds are held in escrow until the tutoring session has been completed and the dispute window has closed.

  • Tutor earnings become available 24 hours after the scheduled session end time, unless a dispute has been opened within that period.
  • Once available, withdrawals are processed to the tutor's chosen payout method (bank account or Stripe-supported method) within 3–5 business days, depending on the payment provider and bank.

  • If a valid refund is approved after a tutor's payout has already been released, UniSkilled reserves the right to recover the refunded amount from the tutor's future earnings or to otherwise seek reimbursement where permitted by law.

Session Fees

UniSkilled enforces a minimum session price of €25 per hour. Tutors may not list sessions below this rate. This minimum ensures quality standards and reflects the true cost of university-level tutoring.

Tutors set their own hourly rates, displayed in their chosen currency on their profile. The rate shown is the tutor's fee for the session. UniSkilled may charge a platform service fee in addition to the tutor's rate — where applicable, this will be displayed clearly before you confirm a booking.

Payment Methods

UniSkilled currently supports payment arrangements directly between tutors and students. Supported methods include bank transfer, PayPal, and other methods agreed between the parties. Do not send cash or cryptocurrency.

Invoicing & VAT

Tutors who are registered for VAT in their jurisdiction must charge VAT to students as required by applicable law. UniSkilled does not automatically collect or remit VAT on behalf of tutors. Tutors operating as businesses must issue proper invoices to students upon request.

Currency

Sessions may be priced in any currency selected by the tutor. The currency displayed on the tutor's profile is the currency in which payment is expected. Exchange rate risk lies with the party making the payment.

Late Payment

Where payment for a confirmed session is not made, the tutor may report the matter to UniSkilled. Repeated non-payment by a student may result in account suspension.

Platform Fees (Future)

UniSkilled reserves the right to introduce a platform service fee in the future. Users will be notified at least 14 days in advance of any such change, and the fee will be clearly displayed during the booking process.
